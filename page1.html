A brief introduction

During a previous bootcamp with Codenation I worked as part of a team to create a text-based adventure game using Python. The criteria we had to meet was that our game must allow the player to make their own choices which will effect gameplay. We mainly did this using if statements and a global variable that increased in value depending on player choices, which would influence which ending of three the player will recieve. I took on the role of team leader, assigning tasks to team members, leading project discussions and recieving input from team members and deciding how we will structure our approach after whittling down ideas. I also wrote all of the story dialogue (minus the endings and boss fights) and coded two levels of the story by myself; The Poison Garden and The Throne Room.

Guardian - My Group Project & Team Leader Role

In regards to the gameplay, the plot revolves around the NPC character named by the player, and you as the player take on the role of their guardian angel, intervining when necessary unknown to anyone else (at first). You must help the player to reclaim their rightful throne from the evil entity that currently rules.
